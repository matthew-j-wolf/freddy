WATER SHORTAGE
By Frederick Wolf

Why is there a shortage of water in the Colorado? Here is a list of reasons.

  • Overuse of water resources
  • Drought
  • The amount of water being used by the states and Mexico exceeds how much water is in the river
  • Snow is melting because of Global Warming
  • A rising population in the river area
  • In the past few years, for every 1 degree temperature rises, flow along the Colorado River dips 9.3%
  • Water is being diverted for irrigation
  • There is a dispute between the United States and Mexico over who has water rights to the Colorado River

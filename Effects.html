EFFECTS OF THE SHORTAGE
By Frederick Wolf

Who, what and how does the shortage of water in the Colorado Rive effect? Here is a list:

  • The River itself who is not reaching the ocean anymore
  • Farmers who do not have enough water to grow crops
  • Native American Tribes who deserve such water
  • Children who use the Colorado river for recreation
  • Plants who are not able to grow
  • Animals who need water to survive
  • 7 States who are in the Colorado's hydrolic basin
  • 40 Million People who get their water from the Colorado, along with
  • Millions More who get food from plants and animals that are dying

So what will happen if we don't find a solution for the Colorado river problem? Some important habitats could disappear, causeing a shortage of food. Many cities and states would lose most or all of their water. Those cities would also loso the electricity the river provides. These Millions of plants, animals, and people could die. Parts of the U.S. and beyond would be forced to find other sources of water.
